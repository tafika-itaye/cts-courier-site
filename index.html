Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our courier services.

You can drop off your parcel at any CTS branch, or request a pickup by calling us or sending a WhatsApp message. We will collect from your location, process the shipment, and deliver to the recipient at the destination branch or their door.
CTS operates across all major cities and towns in Malawi including Blantyre, Lilongwe, Mzuzu, Zomba, Mangochi, Kasungu, Salima, and Dedza. We are continuously expanding our network to serve more locations.
Same-day delivery is available on key routes such as Blantyre-Lilongwe and Blantyre-Zomba. Most inter-city deliveries are completed within 24 hours. Remote locations may take up to 48 hours depending on schedule and accessibility.
Yes. Our e-commerce logistics service includes cash-on-delivery collection. We collect payment from the recipient and remit to the sender according to agreed settlement terms.
Contact our customer service team via WhatsApp or phone with your consignment number. We will provide you with a real-time update on your parcel's status and estimated delivery time.
CTS does not transport hazardous materials, explosives, illegal substances, or perishable goods requiring refrigeration. Fragile items must be properly packaged. Contact us for specific restrictions before booking.
Corporate accounts provide volume-based pricing, monthly invoicing, scheduled pickups, and a dedicated account manager. Complete the corporate account form on this page or contact us to get started. Setup takes 2-3 business days.
